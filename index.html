About the Practice

Accredited care across five specialties

AAIRS Clinic & Troy Sleep Center is a Troy, Michigan practice built around a simple idea: patients deserve thorough testing, board-certified expertise, and treatment plans coordinated across the specialties that actually overlap in real life. Sleep, breathing, and allergic disease rarely sit in tidy boxes — so AAIRS doesn't either.

The sleep lab is accredited by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine, and the interpreting physicians are board certified in Sleep Medicine. Patients can complete in-lab studies at the Troy or Sterling Heights locations, or use a home sleep test device shipped directly from the clinic. AAIRS also evaluates and treats patients seeking allergy and immunology testing, asthma, and pulmonary conditions.

The clinic accepts most major insurance plans and coordinates with local durable medical equipment providers, dentists, and ENT specialists so the right plan — CPAP, oral appliance, immunotherapy, or inhaled therapy — fits the patient, not the other way around.
